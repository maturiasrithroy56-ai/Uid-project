Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham 

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amaravati is a premier, NAAC A++ accredited multi-disciplinary campus located in Andhra Pradesh's capital region.

It offers research-intensive undergraduate and postgraduate programs, focusing on engineering, data science, and management.

The 200-acre lush green campus emphasizes sustainability and modern infrastructure, including advanced laboratories.
